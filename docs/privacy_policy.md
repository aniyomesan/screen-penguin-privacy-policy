## English

### 1. Overview

ScreenPenguin ("the App") helps users organize screenshots on iPhone.  
We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling data transparently.

### 2. Data We Process

- Screenshot/photo access data: The App reads photo library assets you allow it to access in order to display and organize screenshots.
- Local app data: Cleanup history, kept-item records, language/theme settings, and ad display counters are stored on your device.
- Analytics and advertising data (via third-party SDKs): The App uses Firebase Analytics and Google Mobile Ads. These services may collect device/app interaction data (for example, app events, diagnostics, identifiers, IP address, and ad-related signals) under their own policies.
- Remote version check: The App fetches minimum supported version information from a public GitHub-hosted JSON endpoint to determine whether an update is required.

### 3. How We Use Data

- To provide screenshot sorting, review, and deletion features.
- To store your app preferences and cleanup records on-device.
- To improve app quality and stability through analytics.
- To show ads and manage ad frequency.
- To check app version compatibility.

### 4. Photo Content Handling

- Screenshot image content is processed on your device for core app functionality.
- The App itself does not upload your screenshot image files to our own servers.
- Deletion is executed only after your confirmation through iOS system flow.

### 5. Third-Party Services

- Firebase Analytics: [https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- GitHub (version info hosting): [https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement)

### 6. Data Sharing

- We do not sell your personal data.
- Data processed by third-party SDKs is handled according to each provider's privacy policy.

### 7. Data Retention

- App-managed records are stored locally on your device until you delete them, clear app data, or uninstall the App.
- Third-party retention periods are governed by each provider.

### 8. Your Choices

- You can deny or revoke photo permission at any time in iOS Settings.
- You can stop using the App and uninstall it to remove local app data from your device.

### 9. Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed to children under the age required by applicable law.

### 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date.

### 11. Contact

For privacy inquiries, please contact us at aniyome277@gmail.com.
